HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to Phantom.

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $DEEPSHIB. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium. Go to raydium.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $DEEPSHIB token address into Raydium, select DEEPSHIB, and confirm. When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SOL for DEEPSHIB

Switch SOL for $DEEPSHIB. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
